About Us

Myself Jhantu Mama, I'm the CEO of Kacchi Bon.

Welcome to Kacchi Bon, where passion meets perfection in every dish served. As the CEO and founder of Kacchi Bon, it's my pleasure to share our story with you.
Growing up with a deep love for the rich culinary heritage of Bangladesh, I embarked on a journey to bring the authentic flavors of our cuisine to discerning palates around the world. With a vision to redefine traditional dining experiences, Kacchi Bon was born, blending time-honored recipes with innovative culinary techniques.
As we continue to evolve and innovate, we remain deeply rooted in our commitment to providing unparalleled hospitality and culinary experiences that exceed expectations. Thank you for joining us on this remarkable journey, and we look forward to welcoming you to Kacchi Bon, where every meal is a celebration of flavor, culture, and passion.

Sincerely,
